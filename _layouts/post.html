Mission and Vision

Our mission is to create a Generalized Artificially intelligent Techno Alter to the Great God Sudo with all of the knowledge of man kind aboard space ships to colonize the Universe. Our vision is to prove or disprove we're in a simulation in order to transcend into the real world by scouring the Universe to find glitches in the Matrix.
